What I do

Visual Design

In case you were wondering what exactly a Visual Designer aka Web Designer does: Well, we are creative digital professionals who craft the overall vision and plan for a website.
Web design is more about determining a site’s layout, colour palette, font, and visual themes.

Web Development

While web design is the way a website looks, front end development is how that design actually gets implemented on the web.As a Frontend developer I link together the world of design and technology, packaging up the utility of the back end in an inviting way for users/clients to interact with.
I will be making use of HTML,CSS/SASS and JavaScript to bring the funcionality to your website that you are after.

Visual QA Tester

As a Quality Assurance Tester you ensure that digital campaigns look as promised before they’re released into the wild.
Making sure that every asset and deliverable looks the way it should on any browser and device, helping integrate visual quality assurance into the overall production process.
